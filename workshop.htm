Workshop Invitation

The Simons Workshop on The Economics of Networks focuses on economic models for the spread of (mis-)information and adoption of opinions or actions, as well as the importance of the underlying network structure.

  • 9.15-10am PT: Asu Ozdaglar, EECS, MIT
  • 10-10.45am PT: Evan Sadler, Economics, Columbia
  • 10.45-11.15am PT: coffee break with networking
  • 11.15-noon PT: Dean Eckles, Marketing, MIT Sloan

The virtual workshop session will take place on Gather.town in the academic metaverse in the seminar building.
